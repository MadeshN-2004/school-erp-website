Our Features

Student Information Management

The School ERP solution provides a comprehensive Student Information Management system that allows schools to maintain detailed records of each student.

This includes personal details, academic history, attendance, health records, and disciplinary actions. With a centralized database, administrators, teachers, and parents can access real-time information, ensuring transparency and effective communication.

This feature simplifies the enrollment process, streamlines data entry, and provides a secure repository for student data.

Timetable Management

The Timetable Management module helps schools create and manage class schedules efficiently.

It automates the scheduling process, considering factors such as teacher availability, classroom resources, and subject requirements.

The system ensures optimal utilization of resources and prevents scheduling conflicts. Teachers and students can access their timetables online, making it easy to stay informed about class timings and changes. This feature enhances organizational efficiency and minimizes the administrative burden associated with timetable management.

Attendance Tracking

Attendance tracking is a crucial feature of the School ERP solution.

It enables teachers to mark attendance quickly and accurately, either manually or using automated systems such as biometric scanners.

The system generates reports on student attendance, highlighting patterns of absenteeism and punctuality.
